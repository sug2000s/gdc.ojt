TITLE: Black Holes
TOPIC: black holes
LANG: en
---
A black hole is a region of spacetime with gravity so strong that not even light can escape.
Its boundary is called the event horizon.
Supermassive black holes are often found at the centers of galaxies.
Indirect evidence includes:
- Motions of nearby stars
- X-rays from accretion disks
- Gravitational waves from black hole mergers
RAG note:
- Keywords: event horizon, accretion disk, gravitational waves.
