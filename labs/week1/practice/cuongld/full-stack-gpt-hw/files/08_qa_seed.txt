Q: How old is the universe?
A: About 13.8 billion years.

Q: How many planets are in the Solar System?
A: Eight planets.

Q: What is an event horizon?
A: It is the boundary around a black hole beyond which light cannot escape.

Q: What evidence supports black holes?
A: Star motions, X-ray emissions from accretion disks, and gravitational waves.
