TITLE: Galaxies and Large-Scale Structure
TOPIC: galaxies
LANG: en
---
A galaxy is a system of stars, gas, dust, and dark matter bound by gravity.
The Milky Way is the galaxy that contains our Solar System.
Three common galaxy types are:
- Spiral
- Elliptical
- Irregular
Galaxies form clusters and superclusters.
