TITLE: Basic Glossary
TOPIC: glossary
LANG: en
---
Big Bang: a model describing the hot, dense early universe.
Dark matter: matter that does not emit light but has gravitational effects.
Dark energy: a component driving accelerated cosmic expansion.
Light-year: the distance light travels in one year.
Astronomical Unit (AU): average Earth-Sun distance.
Redshift: a shift to longer wavelengths as objects move away.
