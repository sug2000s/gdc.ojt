Major milestones:
- 1957: Sputnik 1, first artificial satellite.
- 1969: Apollo 11, first human Moon landing.
- 1998: International Space Station assembly began.
- 2021+: James Webb Space Telescope operations.
Current goals include returning humans to the Moon and missions toward Mars.
