TITLE: Stars and Stellar Life Cycle
TOPIC: stars
LANG: en
---
Stars form from gas and dust clouds in nebulae.
Gravity causes collapse, temperature rises, and nuclear fusion begins.
A star's life cycle depends on its mass:
- Low-mass stars become white dwarfs.
- High-mass stars may explode as supernovae and leave neutron stars or black holes.
The Sun is a G-type star, about 4.6 billion years old.
